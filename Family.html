Chhota Bheem

Chhota Bheem is one of my favourite cartoon characters. He is a small boy who lives in a village named “Dholakpur”. King Indraverma has faith on him and all the villagers also have faith on him. All the children are his friends but his best friends are- Raju, Jaggu, Chutki, Kalia, Dholu and Bholu. Bheem is very intelligent and wise, his stories are very exciting. He solves all problems very easily. Bheem likes to eat ladoo. He can fight with four dacoits very easily. He loves all his villagers but he is little bit naughty too. Bheem made one tree house for his monkey friend Jaggu. I like Chota Bheem very much and I want to be a good human being like him.
